Our Research Areas

Laboratory for Decision Support Systems specializes in developing mathematical and software tools, and expert estimation conduction for decision-making support in various areas of human activity.

Scientific activity of Lab DSS is targeted at improving decision-making quality based on expert data in weakly structured domains and expanding the opportunities of existing and new DSS. For this purpose academic and applied research is being conducted in various scientific areas.

DSS Lab develops the key directions and traditions of the academic school founded by Professor Vitaliy G. Totsenko.

  • Decision Support

  • Expert Assessment

  • System Analysis

  • Artificial Intelligence

  • Operation Research

  • Discrete Analysis

  • Knowledge Bases

  • Knowledge Engineering

  • Mathematical Modeling

  • Intelligent Technologies
